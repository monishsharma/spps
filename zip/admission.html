REGISTRATION & ADMISSION PROCEDURE

  • Admission is open to all, irrespective of class, creed, and community.
  • Admission to P.G. is ordinarily given to students who have attained the age of two and a half years at the commencement of the academic year.
  • A child name will not be registered for admission unless the undermentioned information has been provided by the parents.
  • The Birth Registration Certificate in original for verification. No change in the date of birth will be permitted later under any circumstances.
  • The latest Progress Report of the child, if the child has been attending a recognised school. After the above requirements have been completed to the entire satisfaction of the Principal, the child's name will be registered for the session.

ICSE ADMISSION

  • For vacancies available for classes II to IX, if notified through our website, then for such cases applications are accepted from April every year till further notice. Applications are to be addressed to the Principal only. Kindly mention the class you are interested in and your contact numbers with email address very clearly. A copy each of the birth certificate and the latest progress report of the child is to be attached with the application.
    • Incomplete applications will be disqualified.
    • The Last date for submitting the application will be displayed on website sardarpatelpublicschool.com.
    • Late applications will not be entertained.
    • Please mention your email address and contact numbers clearly. In case a vacancy occurs, the school office will contact you.

ISC ADMISSION

  • Applications are to be made only after the Preliminary results are declared and if any vacancy exists. Applications are to be addressed to the Principal, mentioning:
    • Child name
    • School studying in
    • Contact numbers & Email are essential
    • Standard

    Attachments required with the application are :
    • Copy of Birth Certificate.
    • Results of standard IX, X and Preliminary Examinations. (photocopies only)
    • Certificates of co-curricular/extra curricular activities. (photocopies only).
    • Incomplete applications will be disqualified.
    The school will contact you in case of vacancy.

UP BOARD ADMISSION

  • The school runs UP Board classes only for standard 9th to 12th. Applications for admission are to be submitted in the school office. Kindly mention the class you are interested in and the following requirements to be attached therewith.
  • Child name,School,class studying in currently,copy of Birth Certificate,results of latest progress report of standard IX, X and preliminary examinations conducted in his/her school (photocopies only) & certificates of co-curricular/extra curricular activities. (photocopies only) will be needed.Incomplete applications will be disqualified.
  • Admission may be subject to the performance of the child in school Admission Test.
  • The admission of the child shall rest on the sole discretion of the Principal.
  • Submission of student's Transfer Certificate is mandatory, as soon as possible, once the admission is granted. Please mention your email address and contact numbers clearly so that school may contact you for any further information.

ACADEMIC CURRICULUM

  • At Sardar Patel Public School, Education for Life, Excellence in Education and Commitment to a meaningful Education are of prime importance.
    Quality education is provided to each and every child.
    SPPS believes that love for education should be developed for children in the initial years and conditions should be created for them to pursue a positive approach in life.

TEACHING METHODOLOGY

  • A unidirectional teaching method is being increasingly substituted by a multidirectional group practical method. Here the teacher becomes the group co-ordinator initiating work, nurturing students without the phobia of examinations and homework. Emphasis is on development of oral and written expression by way of interactive technology-moulded class sessions. Home assignments are not a carry-over of classwork but oriented towards honing individual talents.

ACCREDITATION

  • Sardar Patel Public School is affiliated with The Council for the ICSE/ISC Examinations, New Delhi which is widely acknowledged for the international quality of its courses and pedagogy it offers in the country and is duly recognized by the Department of Education, Government of NCT Delhi. Sardar Patel Public School follows the ICSE-ISC curriculum in Classes I to XII and uses council prescribed books only and examination patterns in various subjects. We also offer UP Board based curriculum in Classes IX-XII and follows its subjects and examination patterns as prescribed by the government.
