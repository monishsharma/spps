Our Mission

Welcome to Sardar patel Public School

Our vision is to nurture our students to realize their own potential and identify and achieve their goals in an environment of rich tutelage and harmonious learning while keeping alive the spirit of togetherness towards the path of enlightenment. Our aim is to bring forth responsible citizens of the world who make a difference and who will make the parents, school and nation proud at all times.

Our Mission

Our mission is to prepare our children for life by:-

  • Broadening their horizons so that their vision encompasses the whole of mankind.
  • Deepening their thoughts so that they achieve their perfection of mind.
  • Incorporating the essential values of honesty, humility, patriotism, discipline, courage and determination as envisaged by the great unifier, Sardar Patel.
  • Guiding them to face adversities without fear and overcome challenges with confidence.

REGISTRATION & ADMISSION PROCEDURE

  • 1. Admission is open to all, irrespective of class, creed, and community.
    2. Admission to P.G. is ordinarily given to students who have attained the age of two and a half½ years at the commencement of the academic year.
    3. A child's name will not be registered for admission unless the undermentioned information has been provided by the parents.
    • The Birth Registration Certificate in original for verification. No change in the date of birth will be permitted later under any circumstances.
    • The latest Progress Report of the child, if the child has been attending a recognised school. After the above requirements have been completed to the entire satisfaction of the Principal, the child's name will be registered for the session.
Admissions to any other standard – (II- IX)

ICSE ADMISSION

  • For vacancies available for classes II to IX, if notified through our website, then for such cases applications are accepted from April every year till further notice. Applications are to be addressed to the Principal only. Kindly mention the class you are interested in and your contact numbers with email address very clearly. A copy each of the birth certificate and the latest progress report of the child is to be attached with the application.
    • Incomplete applications will be disqualified.
    • The Last date for submitting the application will be displayed on website sardarpatelpublicschool.com.
    • Late applications will not be entertained.
    • Please mention your email address and contact numbers clearly. In case a vacancy occurs, the school office will contact you.
Admissions to standard XI (ISC) for students applying from outside schools

ISC ADMISSION

  • Applications are to be made only after the Preliminary results are declared and if any vacancy exists. Applications are to be addressed to the Principal, mentioning:
    • Child’s name
    • School studying in
    • Contact numbers & Email are essential
    • Standard

    Attachments required with the application are :
    • Copy of Birth Certificate.
    • Results of standard IX, X and Preliminary Examinations. (photocopies only)
    • Certificates of co-curricular/extra curricular activities. (photocopies only).
    • Incomplete applications will be disqualified.
    The school will contact you in case of vacancy.
