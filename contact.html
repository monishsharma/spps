CAREER

We at SPPS take immense pride in our strong teaching faculty and other staff. When we look for new teachers to join us on our journey, besides the obvious requirements of academic excellence and the ability to effectively inspire learning, we look for an attitude that connects with ours. We look for a real love for children, team skills and a passion for knowledge as well as quest for learning.


Candidates may apply with a detailed CV (in .doc/.docx/.pdf format only) mentioning their scholastic achievement and past experience in teaching along with a covering letter explaining the candidates suitability for the post. The same shall be mailed to us at our email sppsjuhibarra@gmail.com or sppsnaubasta@gmail.com or submitted at the reception desk in school premises. For any further inquiry you can contact us over call.


Note: We would request you to send application and CV very carefully as it will not be possible to modify it once it has been submitted. In case you do not have all your information with you currently, we would request you to obtain the same before making your application.
